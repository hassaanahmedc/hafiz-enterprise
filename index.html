Who Are Hafiz Enterprise?

Hafiz Enterprises, established in 1995, is a leading supplier of general items, Medical & Industrial gases & Equipment’s, safety equipment, Mechanical & Power tools, and a wide range of hardware products. With over two decades of experience in the industry, the company has built a reputation for reliability, quality, and timely delivery.

From its humble beginnings, Hafiz Enterprises has expanded its portfolio to include a diverse range of products and services, catering to various sectors including construction, industrial, commercial, and retail. The company’s long-standing presence in the market has enabled it to establish strong relationships with clients, suppliers, and industry partners.

Usman Arain, CEO
